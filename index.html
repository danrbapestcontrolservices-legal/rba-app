Corporate Manual

Document Code: RBA-HR-MAN-FINAL-V19

Section 1. Core Corporate Framework and Prerogative. The Company exercises its lawful right to organize, streamline, and direct all operational and human resource requirements in good faith. This includes establishing job duties, assigning specific equipment, setting deployment criteria, and regulating time and place protocols.

Section 2. Prospectivity, Non-Retroactivity, and Acknowledgment. This Manual is promulgated as a forward-looking corporate charter applicable to all team members. Acknowledging this manual confirms an understanding of operational definitions and performance requirements. To protect organizational harmony, any persistent refusal to align with these prospective standards constitutes serious insubordination.

Section 3. Verification and Suspensive Conditions. The perfection of the employment relationship and regularized tenure is subject to a suspensive condition: the completion of a valid 201 File, including background references, standard NBI clearances, and medical certifications.

Section 1. Onboarding Medical Protocol. Given that pest control involves high physical endurance and safe chemical handling, candidates must establish physical fitness. The Company requires a standard "Fit-to-Work" clearance from a licensed physician.

Section 2. Property Protection & Right to Search. To safeguard proprietary chemical formulation balances, high-value specialized equipment, and client data assets, the Company maintains a transparent property defense policy. The Company reserves the right to conduct standard, respectful inspections of bags, lockers, personal effects, and company vehicles upon entry and exit of facilities or client areas. Cooperation is mandatory.

Section 1. Flexible Work Arrangements. The Company implements a Compressed Workweek (CWW) and Flexible Work Arrangement to accommodate operational realities.

Section 2. Field Deployment Boundaries. Compensable field working hours strictly commence upon actual documented arrival at the first assigned client site and strictly terminate upon actual completion and departure from the final assigned client site for the day. Travel intervals from an employee's personal residence or assigned barracks to the first site are treated as standard personal transit time and are non-compensable.

Section 4. Stay-In Facilities. The Company subsidizes utility costs (water and electricity) up to a maximum cap of Php 1,500.00 per month per facility unit. Any utility expenses incurred beyond this threshold shall be billed to and split equally among the residing employees via automated payroll adjustment.

Salary Structure: For team members using their personal vehicles, it is explicitly covenanted that your contractually agreed Salary is fully integrated and inclusive of all gasoline expenses, routine vehicle maintenance, and wear-and-tear.

13th Month: Calculated as one-twelfth of the total basic salary actually earned during the calendar year.

Premium Paid Leaves: 7-Day Sick/Vacation Leave granted annually upon regularization. 3-Day Paid Bereavement Leave granted immediately for immediate family loss.

Cash Advances: Must be fully liquidated with legitimate receipts within 48 hours.

Anti-Moonlighting: Engaging in side-line businesses or privately offering services to the Company's active or potential clients represents a direct conflict of interest. Such acts will result in immediate termination.

The RBA Playbook

Document Code: RBA-HR-MAN-FINAL-V19[cite: 1]

The Core Goal: The Company exercises its lawful right to organize, streamline, and direct human resource frameworks in good faith[cite: 1].

Your 201 File: Tenure and contractual fulfillment remain condition-dependent upon the precise completion and submission of an employee's 201 file records[cite: 1].

Health First: Pest control requires endurance. Employees are required to submit a "Fit-to-Work" clearance from a licensed physician[cite: 1].

Transparency: To protect assets, the Company reserves the right to conduct standard, respectful inspections of bags, lockers, personal effects, and company vehicles upon entry and exit[cite: 1].

Work Setup: We implement a Compressed Workweek (CWW) and Flexible Work Arrangement to accommodate operational realities[cite: 1].

When Does Pay Start? Compensable field hours strictly commence upon actual documented arrival at the first assigned client site and strictly terminate upon actual completion and departure from the final assigned client site[cite: 1]. Travel from personal residence to the first site is non-compensable personal transit time[cite: 1].

Stay-In Perks: Stay-in facility standard utility costs are subsidized to a threshold cap of Php 1,500.00 monthly per unit; excess numbers split evenly across residents[cite: 1].

13th Month: Task-based personnel 13th-month pay is strictly calculated as one-twelfth of the total basic salary actually earned during the calendar year[cite: 1].

Paid Time Off: Welfare parameters include an integrated 7-Day annual Sick/Vacation leave block and a 3-Day paid Bereavement Leave structure[cite: 1].

Vales (Cash Advances): All operational cash advances must be fully liquidated with legitimate, BIR-compliant receipts within forty-eight (48) hours of issuance[cite: 1].

Drugs & Support: Unannounced drug testing procedures align directly with R.A. 9165 parameters; standard refusal constitutes immediate structural insubordination[cite: 1]. The Mental Health Workplace Policy ensures employees facing challenges will be supported by a licensed psychiatrist[cite: 1].

Struggling? If evaluation falls below standard, the employee is placed on a supportive 30-day PIP[cite: 1].

Zero Tolerance: Engaging in side-line businesses ("moonlighting") or privately offering pest control services to the Company's clients is a severe breach of trust resulting in immediate termination[cite: 1]. Bypassing internal channels to defame the Company on social media constitutes Cyber Libel[cite: 1].

Resignations: Submit a 15-day written notice for voluntary resignation[cite: 1]. Final pay processed within DOLE-mandated 30-day period[cite: 1].

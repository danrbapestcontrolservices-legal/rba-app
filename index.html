Corporate Manual

Effectivity: July 2026 (Perfected Legal & Welfare Edition)

Employees are required to submit a "Fit-to-Work" clearance from a licensed physician. The Company reserves the right to conduct standard, respectful inspections of bags, lockers, personal effects, and company vehicles upon entry and exit[cite: 1].
Compensable field working hours strictly commence upon actual documented arrival at the first assigned client site and strictly terminate upon actual completion and departure from the final assigned client site[cite: 1]. Travel from personal residence to the first site is non-compensable personal transit time[cite: 1].

Engaging in side-line businesses ("moonlighting") or privately offering pest control services to the Company's clients is a severe breach of trust resulting in immediate termination[cite: 1]. Bypassing internal channels to defame the Company on social media constitutes Cyber Libel[cite: 1].

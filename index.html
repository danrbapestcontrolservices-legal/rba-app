Corporate Manual

Document Code: RBA-HR-MAN-FINAL-V19

Section 1. Core Corporate Framework. The Company exercises its lawful right to organize, streamline, and direct all operational requirements in good faith.

Section 2. Prospectivity. Acknowledging this manual confirms an understanding of operational definitions. Persistent refusal to align constitutes serious insubordination.

Section 3. Verification. Employment is subject to a suspensive condition: the completion of a valid 201 File, including background references and medical certifications.

Section 1. Medical Protocol. Candidates must establish physical fitness via a "Fit-to-Work" clearance from a licensed physician.

Section 2. Property Protection. The Company reserves the right to conduct standard, respectful inspections of bags, lockers, personal effects, and company vehicles upon entry and exit.

Section 2. Field Boundaries. Compensable field hours commence upon actual documented arrival at the first assigned client site and terminate upon departure from the final client site. Travel from personal residence to the first site is non-compensable.

Section 4. Stay-In Facilities. The Company subsidizes utility costs up to Php 1,500.00 per month per facility unit. Excess is split among residing employees.

Salary Structure: Contractually agreed Salary is inclusive of all gasoline expenses, vehicle maintenance, and wear-and-tear for personal vehicles used.

Premium Leaves: 7-Day Sick/Vacation Leave annually. 3-Day Paid Bereavement Leave for immediate family loss.

Cash Advances: Must be fully liquidated with legitimate receipts within 48 hours.

Anti-Moonlighting: Privately offering services to the Company's clients is a severe breach of trust resulting in immediate termination.

Annual Certification

Answer the 10 randomized questions below based on the RBA Corporate Manual.

At the heart of our operations is a profound commitment to the individuals who execute our vision daily. We provide premium employee protections and generous company welfare benefits—including enhanced leaves and structured health policies—that demonstrate our commitment to your stability.

The Company exercises its lawful right to organize, streamline, and direct all operational and human resource requirements in good faith. This includes establishing job duties, assigning specific equipment, setting deployment criteria, and regulating time and place protocols.

The perfection of the employment relationship and regularized tenure is subject to a suspensive condition: the completion of a valid 201 File.

Candidates must establish physical fitness. The Company requires a standardized Pre-Employment Medical Examination (PEME). Employees are only required to submit a "Fit-to-Work" clearance or restriction note from a licensed physician.

The Company reserves the right to conduct standard, respectful inspections of bags, lockers, personal effects, and company vehicles upon entry and exit of facilities or client areas.

The Company implements a Compressed Workweek (CWW) and Flexible Work Arrangement to accommodate operational realities.

Compensable time includes required reporting, briefing, loading, equipment or PPE issuance, Company transport from a required dispatch point, travel between client sites, controlled waiting, preparation, cleanup, decontamination, and required reports.

Ordinary home-to-first-site and last-site-to-home travel is non-compensable only when no duties are performed and the employee is not under Company control.

Employees assigned to Company-owned vehicles must not disconnect, disable, cover, alter, damage, delete footage from, or otherwise interfere with the dashcam or its wiring, memory card, GPS, settings, or related equipment. Any malfunction, damage, missing memory card, recording interruption, or irregularity must be reported immediately to the Supervisor, Operations Head, or HR.

The Company may provide shared stay-in facilities where operationally necessary and subsidizes water and electricity up to Php 1,500.00 per month per facility unit.

Contractually agreed Salary or Daily Task Rate is fully integrated and inclusive of all gasoline expenses, routine vehicle maintenance, and operational wear-and-tear.

Rank-and-file workers (including those under a Compressed Workweek or Task-Based setup) are entitled to 13th-month pay.

Leaves: 7-Day Sick/Vacation Leave: Granted annually upon regularization to provide our team with ample time to rest and recuperate. 3-Day Paid Bereavement Leave: Granted immediately to support regular employees during the loss of an immediate family member.

Vales: Operational cash advances must be liquidated with legitimate receipts and any unused funds within forty-eight (48) hours, unless Finance grants a documented extension.

RBA shall maintain a written Drug-Free Workplace Policy and Program under R.A. 9165 and DOLE D.O. 53-03. RBA prohibits gender-based sexual harassment, workplace bullying, and violence.

Employees owe their professional dedication exclusively to the Company. Engaging in side-line businesses ("moonlighting") or privately offering pest control or waterproofing services to the Company's active or potential clients represents a direct conflict of interest, a violation of the Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), and a serious breach of trust. Proven violations may result in disciplinary action, up to and including dismissal, and lawful recovery of damages, after investigation and due process.

Employees are encouraged to use the Grievance Committee or HR for prompt internal resolution.

Submit your resignation letter to HR exactly 15 days prior to your last day.

Separation Pay is provided only if the Company initiates separation due to Authorized Causes such as business closure, redundancy, or retrenchment. Employees who voluntarily resign or are separated due to disciplinary Just Causes (e.g., severe infractions, failing PIP) are not legally entitled to Separation Pay.

Final pay, including prorated 13th-month pay and other amounts due, shall generally be released within thirty (30) calendar days from separation or termination.

Field SOPs: Ask the Client or Guard to record and sign the site Time-In. Submit legitimate receipts and return any unused funds to Finance within 48 hours, unless an extension is documented. If you fall ill, text or call your supervisor 2 hours before your shift so we can adjust operations. On your last day, peacefully surrender Company property using Annex J.
